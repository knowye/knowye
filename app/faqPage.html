faq

 
have a comment or question not answered below? send it to us. we'd love to hear from you.

what is knowye?
know what you eat, knowye, is a food centric guest engagement platform for restaurants.

is the knowye app free?
yes.

will knowye sell your email address or send you spam?
no, that's not cool.

what information may be shared with restaurants?
information that identifies you will NOT be shared with anyone. knowye will share your ratings anonymously to help the restaurant improve or to help other diners find the best dishes.

why is the menu not showing any items?
try turning off your preferences at the top of the menu. you will not see items that don't meet the preferences you set in your profile.

i clicked but nothing happened.
try clicking again.

does selecting an item place an order?
no, you have to tell your server what you selected.

does knowye show me my bill?
knowye shows you an estimate of your bill, including tax, and has a tip calculator, but your actual bill may be different.
